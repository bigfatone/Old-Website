Why Focus on Visual Design?

I believe that there can and should be some overlap in UI and UX when factoring the user into the end iteration. For example, if a company's product looks visually appealing (UI), then users are more likely to give it a try at least once. However, if a product is strong at its core functions (UX), then customers are more likely to keep coming back to your product time and time again. To me, this means that both approaches have their uses, and both approaches cannot be neglected if I want to provide the full experience as a UX practitioner.

About This Project

DailyUI is a free 100 day long project with prompts sent via email on weekdays. These daily prompts range from a checkout page to redesigning DailyUI's own landing page. According to their website, UI designers from Netflix, Disney, Samsung, and many other companies have taken part in creating quick mockups of user interfaces.
